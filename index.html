Our Story

Opened on May 24, 2022, Jay Natraj Foods is located at 448-1 Soi Charoenkrung 47, Surawong Road, Siphraya, Bangrak, Bangkok 10500. We specialize in Indian vegetarian catering, offering thali meals and buffet options for all occasions.

Our thali offerings include: • Gujarati Thali • Marwadi Thali • South Indian Thali • Punjabi Thali

We also serve a variety of Indian street food, including chat items.
